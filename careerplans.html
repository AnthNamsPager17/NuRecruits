Lachlan Jensen

Lachlan’s ideal job is to become a Network Engineer. His ideal profession has remained the same throughout the duration of this course and his drive to achieve his goals is only increasing. The career plan that will enable Lachlan to become a network engineer involves goals such as completing a degree in Information Technology, completing CCNA R&S certification, gaining experience working at a Managed Service Provider (MSP) and overall building a vast set of skills across all fields of information technology. Lachlan’s career plan shares many similarities to other career plans within his group, such as Jeremy Baker’s plan to become a cloud engineer and Anthony Namroud’s plan to become a cybersecurity engineer. Network engineer, Cloud engineer, and Cybersecurity engineer are all positions that require an extensive level of networking knowledge and experience. The paths to achieve each of these career goals share many similarities, such as all career plans beginning with earning a degree in IT and all of them requiring many years of experience working in the field of networking or at an MSP. Since the journey to achieve these goals share so many similarities it shows that the skills, we develop for our ideal job can be useful across many different professions, not just the one we plan for now, allowing our ideal jobs to evolve over time and adapt to the everchanging IT landscape.
