Lead Software Engineer

Position Title: Lead Software Engineer
Type of employ: Permanent Full Time
Expected hours: 40 hours/week
Salary: TBA
Location: RMIT University Melbourne City Campus

At nuRecruITs we are passionate about using upcoming technology to save lives. We are looking for a full-time software engineer, who shares our passion, to join our team. We are currently in the field of medical drone development and are developing prototypes to demonstrate our teams’ goals.

Our team of 6 is newly formed, and with government contracts in our vision, we are excited for what the future holds. As our lead software engineer you will manage the software development team and teach the team the skills that will drive our company moving forward.

Our development has primarily been based around the python programming language but are looking to adapt to any language that allows the team to achieve its goals.

Key Responsibilities

As our team’s lead software engineer you will be responsible for:

  • Managing the software development team
  • Daily meetings
  • Designing functioning code to meet company requirements
  • Debugging and testing
  • Documentation and reporting
  • Teaching other members of the team best practices
  • Researching latest technology trends
  • Meeting deadlines
  • Problem-solving
  • Programming in Python
  • Introducing new programming languages to better suit our goals

Position Requirements

A successful applicant will be able to lead and direct the software development team and give valuable insight into the development of not only the current project, but projects in the future. Our lead software engineer will also require the following:

  • A degree in computer science or a similar degree
  • 3+ years as a Software Engineer
  • Exceptional knowledge of the Python language
  • Experience with other programming languages such as Java, C, C++, C#, MATLAB, HDLs, LISP
  • Experience programming with drones or robots
  • Exception team management and leadership skills

What's in it for you?

You will have the opportunity to work with state-of-the-art hardware and software, alongside a passionate team that is stiving to succeed. We are able to offer job security for the future and your salary will reflect the hard work you put in. Our team are able to provide exciting opportunities to work with government agencies to actually make a change in the world. At nuRecruITs the work you conduct will be actively saving lives, making it one of the most rewarding jobs possible.
