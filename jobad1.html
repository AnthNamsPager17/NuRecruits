Information Security Analyst

nuRecruITs

Position Title: Information Security Analyst
Type of employ: Permanent Full Time
Expected hours: 38 hours/week
Salary: TBA
Location: RMIT University Melbourne City Campus

Key Responsibilities

Our project will be utilising both drone technologies and modern software programs, in response your key responsibilities will require you to:

  • Remain up to date on the latest security trends and technologies.
  • Analyse security issues thoroughly and identify the root cause.
  • Document and measure security breaches.
  • Implement firewalls and essential data encryption programs to safeguard organisation vital data.
  • Suggest security enhancement methods to the organisation management and IT staff.
  • Implement both internal and external security audits.
  • Create a disaster recovery plan that would transfer data to other locations and restores IT functioning immediately after a disaster.
  • Train company staff on better security protocols

Position Requirements

A successful applicant will have:

  • 3+ years previous experience in Information security or similar roles
  • Bachelor of Computer Science or equivalent
  • Ethical hacker certification
  • Excellent Communications skills
  • Detail oriented and analytical mindset
  • Highly motivated
  • Problem solving skills

nuRecruITs are a small organisation with key members Australia wide, communication skills in this position amongst staff is key. As well, our main project will involve the use of medical drones carrying expensive and valuable equipment, this position would require a skilled problem solver to tackle the unique dilemma of having range-controlled technologies in need of cyber protection from ill parties.

What's in it for you?

nuRecruITs offer the exciting opportunity to work with state-of-the-art modern technologies, in the pursuit of life saving emergency response actions. Our smaller team size presents you with increased sway and weight in team meetings and discussions, furthermore, providing greater direct contact with all other team members in our organisation.as well as greater flexibility and autonomy than other “big tech” companies. we can offer a Full-Time position, in a union aligned work force, at a starting salary above award to the person who enters our outfit.
