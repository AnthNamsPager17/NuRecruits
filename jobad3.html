Drone Electrical Engineer

Position Title: Drone Electrical Engineer
Type of employ: Permanent Full Time
Expected hours: 38 hours/week
Salary: TBA
Location: RMIT University Melbourne City Campus

nuRecruITs are a start-up company that is aiming to deliver out product throughout Australia. With a start of reach of a metropolitan city such as Melbourne and Sydney we are aiming to use our drones to deliver lifesaving equipment faster than emergency services can arrive at the destination.

Here at nuRecruITs we are a close team who operate in a timely fashion, with team communication being an integral part of our day-to-day operations.

Key Responsibilities

The Electrical Engineer will be responsible for developing and delivering an in house designed drone that is capable of traveling at high speeds for a minimum 6 minutes, the drone will need to be capable of breaking glass, using GPS co-ordinates, and live cameras operational for emergency services.

This drone will need to be capable of being operation for at minimum 1 hour before needing to be charged, have high quality cameras operational, and be able to carry up to 10kg in weight each time.

You will be tasked with delivering and improving our drones with the expectation of improving the quality of life of our drones while sticking to strict constraints on how much money can be spent on development.

In this role you will need to communicate with our lead programmer, lead app developer and key stakeholders within our business, being able to work in a close team environment is a must have.

You will be responsible for sourcing the materials you require, via using the correct business routes. In addition to this you will be required to present your development each month at our monthly team progress meetings and be able to show sufficient amount of work has been completed.

In this role you work autonomously towards the business’s goals without the need for direct daily supervision.

Our drones are constantly developing, we are aiming to have a drone that can break glass, apply a defibrillator, and perform chest compressions on its own without human interference.

Ideal Candidate

  • Our ideal candidate will show they have what it takes to work both autonomously and within a team environment.
  • You must have a university degree in Electrical Engineering, Robotics Engineering or Computer Science.
  • Highly motivated.
  • Excellent thinking and creative skills
  • Strong team player.
  • Ability to troubleshoot electrical, software, and associated equipment problems.
  • Outstanding computer skills, using windows associated programs.
  • Hands on skills with a passion for new technology and its applications.
  • Programming skills in using Python, JavaScript, C++, and C#.

When applying for this role you must submit a detailed resume with your degree clearly listed.

No cover letter submitted = No interview.

What is in this for you?

At nuRecruITs we have weekly team lunches with monthly full day meetings going over the direction of the business needs and requirements, the direction forward and how we can accommodate each other’s goals.

We are open to all ideas that help us achieve our goal of increasing the rate in which defibrillators are used prior to an ambulance arriving.

If you become a part of our growing start-up, you will not be disappointed with a growing salary as the business grows, the opportunity to work on state-of-the-art equipment while contributing to save humans lives, and a fantastic team environment where every team member is valued and heard.
