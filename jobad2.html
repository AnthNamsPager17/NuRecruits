IT Program Manager

Position Title: IT Program Manager
Type of employ: Contract/Temp
Expected hours: 40 hours/week
Salary: TBA
Location: RMIT University Melbourne City Campus

nuRecruITs are an IT start-up with a government contract to bring Australia one step closer to the future age of autonomous transport and logistics. They have an exciting opportunity for an experienced IT Project Manager to head up our cutting-edge nationwide technology rollout.

Key Responsibilities

The project utilises the latest advances in wearable technology, autonomous drones, app, and web development to deliver critical services to improve the lives of all Australians.

The role will run from ideation to completion, thus will involve all aims, scope, and policy preparation. Demonstration of project management theory, development methodologies expertise and past experience with successful projects of similar scale and budget is desirable.

High level stakeholder engagement within Federal, State and Local Government and Emergency Services Organisations. Building strong relationships with external and internal stakeholders to ensure smooth and efficient service delivery within a novel technology space. The role will involve liaising with technical and engineering teams and providing regular status and budget reports to Government agencies.

The role will oversee end-to-end consumer experience, including internal development, UX, data analytics and product management. You will manage multiple IT projects of varying complexity and scale to ensure smooth rollout of a game-changing technology and global first initiative. Projects must be completed on schedule, to budget and with maximum positive public relations impact.

Projects will include the rollout of new systems and applications, as well as large scale infrastructure development across urban and rural Australia. Technical knowledge and experience is essential, alongside strong existing relationships within government agencies.

About You

You will share the RecruITs vision for the future of Australia, a future of greater sustainability, efficiency and autonomy, freeing humans to spend more of their time being good humans while reducing our footprint on our precious Earth.

Demonstrated ability to handle large budgets and multiple, complex projects across varying time frames is required.

The ability to communicate with technical and non-technical stakeholders across all levels of the program is essential. Demonstrated skills in problem solving, delegation and team building are a must. Show us a history of inspiring confidence in the people around you, facilitating transparency in business processes and empowering your teams.

You will dive passionately into the full scope of the project, developing a deep understanding of its motivations and aims, and the various steps required for completion. Your implementation methodology will be inspired and sound, ensuring this radical project is seen through to fruition and protected from the possibility of a sceptical public.

You will have tertiary qualifications in IT and Project Management, and an extensive and varied professional history of successful project implementations.

You are a self-starter with abundant energy and passion for information technology, and a vision to see the limits of technological implementation pushed back to facilitate generational change.

What's In It for You?

A global first of its kind project, this is the chance to bring together the cutting edge of several technology fronts to synergise a world-changing project that will deliver real positive impacts to Australian lives.

Bring your diverse experience and established relationships to push IT into new frontiers. A long and rewarding contract (2+ years) with autonomy, oversight, and flexibility. Work for a positive and enthusiastic IT start-up and help shape the future of this open-minded and forward-thinking organisation.

The role will be based in Sydney, with requirement to frequently travel to urban and rural areas of Australia. Some flexibility to work remotely is possible.

If you are interested in this unique and fantastic opportunity, then we are interested in hearing from you! Tell us how you want to shape the future of Australia.
