Tools

Our group communicated about github pushes and pulls via a Microsoft teams chat, with all of us in it. Our group has communicated via two weekly meetings in which we would have our time to talk about what we have accomplished up until that meeting. The delegations of each task were done in week 1 and we allowed people to complete their task at their own pace, so long as they had completed a certain percentage each week. This ended up being 33%, 66% and 100% for weeks 2, 3 and 4 respectively. Week 5 was for everything being finalised on our website and in our PDFs that would be uploaded for our assignment submission. The bulk of our github work was done during the final week (week 5) with everyone contributing towards the upload, however the bulk of the uploads was done by two team members, this being our website developers. Our groups work was not completely finalised until the final meeting in week five, meaning we still had to review more word over the final three days before submission, however our PDFs for upload were completed prior to transferring everything over to our website which helped significantly when uploading to github.
