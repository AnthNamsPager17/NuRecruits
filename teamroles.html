Our Job Roles

Nicholas Richards’ Role – Data Analyst and Marketing Assistant

I have been assigned the job title Data Analyst and Marketing Assistant. This role entails the creation of 6-8 detailed and compelling case studies that will support the business objectives of our IT Project. The case studies will demonstrate the overwhelming benefits of drone delivery of life saving equipment using real world or realistic hypothetical scenarios. They will need to include comparative mapping, analysis with calculated current response times, and an in-depth analysis of this raw data to provide insights to inform decision making regarding the viability of the project.

This data will be fed into our marketing plan to present to government and/or industry in order to secure funding for infrastructure as well as policy and licensing approval. I will be responsible for collecting the data, instigating the case studies, undertaking analysis, and presentation of useful insights utilising visual and auditory presentation aids and communication skills.
